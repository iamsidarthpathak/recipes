Biryani

Description

Biryani is a flavorful, aromatic, and iconic rice-based dish originating from the Indian subcontinent, known for its layered preparation with meat (chicken, lamb, goat, etc.) or vegetables, and a medley of spices. It's a popular celebratory dish and widely available as street food
