Simple Pasta

Description

A light and easy pasta dish with garlic and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 200 g pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (chopped)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until soft; drain.
  2. Heat olive oil, saut√© garlic.
  3. Add tomato sauce, season with salt and pepper.
  4. Toss pasta in the sauce and serve hot.
